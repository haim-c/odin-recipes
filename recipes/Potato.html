Potato Recipe

“Oi, you overcooked lump of starch! You’re supposed to be fluffy, not a soggy mess! I’m a potato, for crying out loud—versatile, humble, perfect! Mash me right, roast me golden, fry me crispy! If you can’t handle that, get out of the bloody kitchen!”

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Rub potato in Oil
  2. Cook potato
